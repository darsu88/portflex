my.principles

1. usability

design with the user in mind, making the user experience intuitive and easy to use

2. simplicity

keep the design simple and clear to reduce complexity and increase usability

3. visual.hierarchy

design techniques highlight important product elements and attract users' attention

4. eficiency

make it easy for users to complete tasks and objectives with as few steps and as little time as possible

Hoovey

Hoovey allows you to create a budget and track your expenses, so you can identify areas where you can cut back on spending. You can also set savings goals and monitor your progress towards achieving them. The app even helps you plan for unexpected expenses by setting aside a portion of your savings each month.
